Creative Horizons Fund

Our mission here at Creative Horizons Fund is to promote artistic vision, support creativity, and aid artists in their vision.

At Creative Horizons Fund, our vision is to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive arts community by empowering artists at every stage of their journey. We strive to provide vital financial support that enables individuals to explore, experiment, and express their creativity without barriers. Through our grants, we aim to spark innovation, foster artistic growth, and inspire a world where creativity is celebrated as a fundamental force for cultural and social transformation.

Our commitment to the arts

Since its inception, Creative Horizons Fund has been a driving force in bringing innovative and diverse artistic projects to life. By supporting both emerging and established artists through grants, the organization has fostered a wide range of creative endeavors across various mediums, including visual arts, performance, digital media, public art, and more.

The Funds commitment to promoting public art has played a significant role in transforming communities, making art more accessible to the general public, and creating shared cultural experiences. Through murals, installations, sculptures, and interactive exhibitions, Creative Horizons Fund has helped bring art into public spaces, where it can spark conversation, inspire reflection, and enrich the urban landscape.

In addition to traditional forms of art, the organization has championed experimentation with new technologies and digital platforms, supporting projects that push the boundaries of art and storytelling. This includes immersive experiences, multimedia performances, and digital installations, allowing artists to connect with audiences in innovative and unexpected ways.

Creative Horizons Fund has also prioritized inclusivity and diversity in its grant-making, ensuring that artists from all backgrounds—regardless of experience, medium, or community—have the resources to bring their creative visions to fruition. Whether through funding for collaborative projects, experimental works, or community-based art initiatives, the Fund has been a vital catalyst for artistic expression and cultural dialogue.

Through these efforts, Creative Horizons Fund continues to create a ripple effect of inspiration, empowering artists to explore new ideas, engage with their communities, and contribute to the broader cultural landscape.
