Our Past Grants

Gallery Projects

The Creative Horizon Fund has supported a diverse range of artistic projects, including solo exhibitions, gallery shows, and public art installations. By empowering artists through their chosen mediums, the fund has helped bring their visions to life. One such exhibition in Soho showcased the work of a talented artist, marking a pivotal stepping stone in their promising career.

Take to the Stage

This grant empowered the exceptional dance company Syncopated Souls to present their meticulously choreographed yet fluid movements to a captivated audience. The company, comprised of both seasoned dancers and fresh talent, brought a dynamic blend of experience and innovation to the stage. Their showcase illuminated not only their unique artistic vision but also introduced the world to the exciting new faces of dance, marking a memorable milestone in their journey.

In this capstone project, Pace University musical theater student Ethan Brooks took the stage to showcase his remarkable talent in a performance of Phantom of the Opera, made possible by a Creative Horizon Fund grant. The production was outstanding, offering Brooks the chance to shine in the spotlight. This pivotal project paved the way for his Broadway debut, where he now performs in the main cast of Little Shop of Horrors!
